# Copy Guardrails

Use this as the voice and positioning baseline for site copy.

## Identity

Primary identity:

- Growth & E-Commerce Executive

Preferred positioning language:

- Marketplace Growth
- Revenue Leadership
- Operating Cadence
- Forecasting
- Margin-Aware Acquisition
- Lifecycle & Retention
- Retail Media
- Executive Reporting
- AI-Enabled Growth Systems

## Preferred Verb Style

Use direct first-person operator language where appropriate:

- I led
- I built
- I scaled
- I improved
- I partnered with
- I helped establish

## Avoid

- "I'm passionate about..."
- "Unlock your growth..."
- "10x your business..."
- "Data-driven marketer..."
- "Growth hacker..."
- "Full-funnel wizard..."
- "Fractional CMO for hire" as the main identity

## Tone

Senior, clear, direct, commercially credible, and operator-led. The site should feel like an executive portfolio, not a generic agency landing page.
